Now we have to find \pr{X<0.5},
\begin{align}
\pr{X<0.5} = \int_{-\infty}^{0.5}{\fn{x}}\,dx\label{g}
\end{align}
Using \eqref{f} in \eqref{g}
\begin{align}
    \pr{X<0.5} &= \int_{0}^{0.5}{2x}\,dx\\
    &= \left[x^2\right]_0^{0.5}\\
    &= (0.5)^2-0\\
  \implies  \pr{X<0.5} &= 0.25
\end{align}
